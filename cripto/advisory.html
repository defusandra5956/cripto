David Sorin

David Sorin Chair's McCarter & English's Venture Capital & Emerging Growth Companies practice, representing privately- and publicly-owned startup, early stage, emerging growth, and middle market technology, tech-enabled and life science enterprises, as well as the investors, executives, and boards of directors who support and lead them. Dave has a long track record of successful representation of growth companies, having been repeatedly recognized by well-known periodicals for a unique combination of legal acumen and sound business judgment, as well as his involvement in emerging technologies from market inception.

With respect to his blockchain, smart contracts, crypto-focus, Dave and his team are recognized as thought leaders in these emerging areas, speaking and publishing articles of interest on these topics for Meetups, FundingPost, Capco and others. Dave has worked on both utility and security tokens, SAFTs (Simple Agreements for Future Tokens issued in compliance with Regulation D), and ICOs (including under Regulation A) and increasingly finds himself engaged in matters in legal areas in addition to corporate, finance and securities, including trusts & estates, contracts law, tax, and intellectual property.

Most recently, Chambers, the leading publication that ranks lawyers and law firms, named Dave one of the top lawyers in the US in the representation of startup, emerging growth and venture capital backed companies. Just last week, INC. Magazine recognized named Dave one of the top lawyers in NY, specifically citing his «client intelligence».
